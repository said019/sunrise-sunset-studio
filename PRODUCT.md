# Sunrise Sunset Product Context

## Product

Sunrise Sunset is a boutique wellness studio in Los Cabos for Sculpt, Surf-Pilates, Yoga, trial classes, special events, and workshops. The platform lets clients discover the studio, create an account, buy packages, book classes, manage reservations, and return to their personal app.

## Register

brand

## Audience

Primary audience: women who want to feel strong, healthy, confident, and cared for. They value wellness, design, personal attention, and movement as part of their lifestyle.

Secondary audience: foreign visitors and residents in Los Cabos who expect a polished, bilingual-friendly, boutique experience that feels easy to trust.

## Brand Feeling

Entering the platform should feel like entering a careful, inspiring, welcoming studio. The emotional tone is boutique, premium, relaxed, energetic, and close. It should feel like a beautiful pause in the day: conscious movement, strength, warm light, and a wellness retreat sensibility.

## Differentiation

Sunrise Sunset combines Sculpt and Surf-Pilates in a coastal boutique environment, with small groups, personalized attention, and an atmosphere inspired by the Los Cabos lifestyle.

## Values

Wellbeing, discipline, community, confidence, conscious movement, balance, and quality.

## Voice

Warm, clear, premium, and close. Spanish first, but with selected English phrases where they feel natural for Los Cabos visitors. Avoid generic wellness cliches and overly loud sales language.

## Surfaces In Scope

Landing page, login, register, and public-facing authentication entry points. The first screen should sell the actual studio experience, not a generic marketing shell.
